How-tos

  1. Set up a video room: Support or Sales.

  2. Post the link(QR) on your web page and you client can click in to enter the room from the page or you can send direct link to your client via SMS Text.

  3. Your client can choose which device you want to enter the room and share video and whiteboard.

  4. If you are not online, your client can leave you message and snapshot. 

  5. You can check your inbox later.
